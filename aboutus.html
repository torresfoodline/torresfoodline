About Company

• Torres Trading Co. Inc. was founded in 1986 at the YTMI Realty Special Economic Zone in Calamba, Laguna.

• Its initial mission was to provide food for the Yazaki – Torres employees. A couple of years after, bakery production was added.

• We expanded operations and started our own shopping center to cater to Yazaki Torres employees

• Presently, Torres Foodline services 18,300 employees of YTMI (14,000), Murata (1,800)

• Honda (2,500) for all their food daily requirements.

• In 2013, Torres Foodline was formed to manage its food manufacturing and food service units :

o Packaged Food

o The Park Cafe and Grill

o The Park Bed and Breakfast

o The Park Events Center

o The Park Food Hub o Shopping Center
